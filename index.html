The Scholar

I am a PhD student in Economics at Heriot-Watt University, specializing in the intersection of microeconometrics and 19th-century history. My passion is unearthing quantitative evidence from forgotten archives to understand how societies self-organized before the modern state.

My primary research involves the digitisation and analysis of thousands of Friendly Society returns. These records reveal a complex web of community-based welfare that provided critical support to millions of people during Britain's rapid industrialization.
